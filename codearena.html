CODING MASTER'S CHALLENGE

Event Focus: Targeting pro coders ready to deploy their knowledge about algorithms and data-structures to solve coding problems of different levels of difficulty.

Day 1

Elimination Round: Participants will be given 4 Coding Questions to test in-depth knowledge of basic algorithms and data structure. Contest will be hosted Online on www.HackerRank.com. Participants need to solve 2 problems to qualify to the final round.

Day 2

Final Round: Participants will be given 6 coding problems with CodeVita contest difficulty level. Contest will be hosted on www.CodeChef.com

Coding Language: C, C++, JAVA and Python.

Rules (same for both days):

  1. Each round is an elimination round.
  2. It will be a team event. A team should have maximum of two students from the same college.
  3. Use of laptops, books, phones or any other electronic device is not allowed and talking to other contestants other than his/her team member may lead to disqualification.
  4. Opening any other website other than the website provided will lead to disqualification.
  5. No restriction in programming language i.e. a contestant can use any language of his/her choice.

CYBER HUNT

Event Focus: Rigorous knowledge of cyber security is the format of capture the flag(CTF) event along with development tweaks. Students have to participate in teams of two and use resources like Google to search for clues/hint in order to solve the questions that test logical & analytical skills.

Day 1

Elimination Round (Buckle Up) : Participating teams will solve a questions to login and be put through a brain-teasing session where they will have to solve the questions within the time limit.

Day 2

Final Round (Epilogue): Shortlisted teams will go through a sound of complex questions.

Challenges:

  • Questions require some sort of programming technique to solve.
  • Decrypting of encrypted messages.
  • “look into” an executable or a binary file or output file and hunt for clues to get the answer.

Rules (same for both days):

  1. Each round is an elimination round.
  2. It will be a team event. A team should have two students from the same college.
  3. You will be allowed to use Google to search for clues/hints in order to solve the questions.
